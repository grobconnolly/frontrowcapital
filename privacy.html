1. Introduction

Front Row Capital ("Front Row Capital," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at frontrow.capital, contact us by email, or engage with our content and advertising on platforms such as LinkedIn.

Information you provide directly to us — for example, when you complete our contact form, email us, or otherwise communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, organization, investor type, and the contents of your messages. We do not collect payment information or process financial transactions through this website.

Information collected automatically when you visit our website, such as log and usage data (how you interact with the site) and device information (IP address, browser type, and operating system). We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies, including analytics and advertising tools, to collect this information.

Information from advertising platforms — if you engage with our advertising on LinkedIn or similar platforms, those platforms may use cookies or pixels to measure and target our campaigns and may share limited engagement information with us.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as website hosting, analytics, email, and advertising partners (for example, Google and LinkedIn); with our professional advisors, such as legal and accounting professionals; and with law enforcement, government officials, or other third parties when required by subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or to comply with legal obligations. 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, update, or delete the information we hold about you; to object to or restrict its processing; to request data portability; and to withdraw your consent.
