Methodology

Player earnings data covers 2012–2042 (MLB salaries from baseball-reference / Spotrac). This simulator models the $8.5M ISA portfolio of the $10M total fund. The fund’s separate $1.5M Finlete equity allocation has its own return profile and is not included in this view—see the Finlete materials for that piece. 30 random players are drawn without replacement from the chosen pool. Each player is acquired for $283K in exchange for 5% of their MLB earnings; together they constitute the full $8.5M ISA portfolio. Each player contributes 5% of their annual MLB earnings to the ISA portfolio, year by year. Management fees ($170K/yr in years 1–3, $42.5K/yr in years 4–12) are charged on the $8.5M ISA portfolio basis and subtracted from collections each year. Distributions follow a European whole-fund waterfall on the ISA portfolio: 100% to LPs until $8.5M of ISA capital is returned, then 80% LP / 20% GP. Net IRR computed via bisection on the resulting LP cash flow stream. Backtest only — past performance is not indicative of future results. Confidential. For accredited investor discussion only.
