The Opportunity

The most reliable asset class you've never been able to own.

The fund provides capital today to the game's most promising young players in exchange for a small percentage of their future Major League earnings. The Top 100 prospects are a remarkably reliable cohort — and historically a deeply mispriced one.

This isn't a theory. The firm that pioneered the model has already deployed $190M+ across 700+ players — proof, at institutional scale, that future-earnings contracts on elite prospects work. Front Row Capital brings that thesis to a focused, LP-friendly fund, powered by our own proprietary signing platform.
