The Context

Minor leaguers earn minimum wage. Even the best ones.

We give them money so they can focus on baseball year-round – nutrition, training, family expenses. Better focus means better careers. Better careers means better returns for us.

The Deal

We cut them a check today. They pay us back from future earnings.

We get a percentage of their MLB salary. We're literally invested in their success. It's an investment, not a loan.

Example
$150K for 3% of future earnings. Breakeven at $5M in career earnings.
The Play

We will back 15+ top 100 prospects over three years.

We spread the risk across multiple athletes from multiple cohorts. Not a bet on one player.
